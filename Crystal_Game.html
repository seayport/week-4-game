Crystal Collector Game


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal,
you will add a specific number of points to your total score.
You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
When the game starts over, new values will be loaded for each crystal.

Click on a Crystal to increase your total!
